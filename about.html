About Us

Our instructors

Our instructors at Toronto Ballet Studio are known for their unparalleled talent and extensive experience in the world of dance. With deep-rooted knowledge and expertise, they bring a level of professionalism that is unmatched in the industry. Each instructor has undergone rigorous training from renowned ballet schools and companies, ensuring they possess an intimate understanding of the art form. Their dedication to honing their craft is evident in the precision and grace with which they move, inspiring students day after day.

Studio Director

Passion meets precision on the ballet stage. Greta Seletskaja, a renowned ballet dancer with 25 years of experience, gracefully commands the spotlight. As the studio director in Toronto, she nurtures aspiring dancers into stars. When not captivating audiences or perfecting her craft, Greta finds solace in hiking alongside her loyal four-legged companion. Fuelling her curious mind, she immerses herself in captivating books and indulges in wanderlust through travel adventures. Join Greta's journey as she continues to inspire through artistry and exploration.

Choreographer

Creativity in motion. Hope Côté, a passionate choreographer at Toronto Ballet Studio, brings charm and grace to the world of ballet. With a lifelong love for dance since childhood, she weaves enchanting stories through her choreography. When not in the studio, you can find her gracefully gliding along the beaches, finding inspiration in every step. Join her on this captivating journey of expression and movement.

Kuras Morris

Coach

Kuras Morris, a dedicated ballet coach at the esteemed Toronto Ballet Studio. Passionate about the art of dance, he shares his expertise with aspiring dancers and inspires them to reach new heights. A hard worker by nature, Kuras can often be found perfecting his craft or guiding students through graceful movements. In his free time, he finds solace in long walks with his furry companion and indulges in the soulful melodies of live jazz concerts. Join him on this enchanting journey through the world of dance and music.
